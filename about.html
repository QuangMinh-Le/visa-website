SHOUT OUT TO ALL VIETNAMESE STUDENTS IN OTTAWA!

VISA (Vietnamese International Student Association) is a non-profit, non-political organization, aiming to maintain a common network for all Vietnamese international students in Ottawa.

Our Mission

We strive to build connections with larger organizations such as universities, colleges, and high schools to support students and create an information network.

What We Do

  • Workshops & Information Sessions on visas, passports, and immigration.
  • Charity & Volunteer Activities to showcase the contributions of Vietnamese students.
  • Maintaining an online community through our Facebook page.

VISA is dedicated to creating a supportive network for Vietnamese international students.
